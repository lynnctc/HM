§1

Why Quality — and the Three Ways It Fails

為什麼談品質——以及它失敗的三種方式

Quality: “the degree to which health services for individuals and populations increase the likelihood of desirable health outcomes and are consistent with current professional knowledge.” Two working questions: are the right things done (effectiveness)? and are things done right (efficiency)? The quality gap is the distance between what research says is optimal and what actually happens — evidence takes an average of 17 years to reach routine clinical practice.

品質：「醫療服務提升個人與族群獲得理想健康結果的可能性、且符合當前專業知識的程度。」兩個工作問句：做的是對的事嗎（效果）？把事做對了嗎（效率）？品質缺口是研究上的最佳實務與實際作為之間的距離——證據平均要 17 年才會走進例行臨床。

Donabedian: Structure → Process → Outcome

Donabedian：結構 → 過程 → 結果

Structure 結構

  • Material and human resources + the facility: staffing numbers, skill levels, certifications, accreditation物力人力與設施：人力數、技能、證照、機構認證

Process 過程

  • The actual delivery and management of care: cleanliness, feeding, treatments, keeping patients safe照護的實際提供與管理：清潔、餵食、治療、防跌防錯

Outcome 結果

  • Resulting status: mortality, morbidity, length of stay, function; also staff turnover, costs最終狀態：死亡率、併發症、住院日、功能；亦含人員流動與成本

§3

Measurement and Variation

測量與變異

Every QI effort begins with a rigorous definition of the concept, a written metric with its unit of measure, and a method that is reliable (consistent across time and raters) and valid (actually measures the concept). Then comes variation: common-cause variation is the natural noise of a process — reduce it by redesigning the process; special-cause variation comes from unusual, infrequent events — detect and eliminate them. Statistical process control (SPC) charts the metric over time with control limits at ±3 standard deviations: intuitive to read, but only flags what is statistically unusual, not what is clinically acceptable.

每個 QI 專案都始於嚴謹的概念定義、含測量單位的書面指標，以及信度（跨時間與評分者一致）與效度（真的測到概念）兼具的方法。接著談變異：共同原因變異是流程的自然雜訊——靠重新設計流程來縮小；特殊原因變異來自異常、少見的事件——要偵測並排除。統計製程管制（SPC）把指標畫成時間序列並設 ±3 個標準差的管制界限：直觀易讀，但它只標記「統計上異常」，不代表「臨床上可接受」。

§4

Three Improvement Engines

三部改善引擎

Continuous quality improvement (CQI): employee teams identify and fix problems in their own work processes — continuously, not as a one-time project. Five dimensions: process focus, customer focus, data-based decisions, employee empowerment, organization-wide impact. Health care's most-used recipe is FOCUS-PDCA (HCA's extension of Shewhart/Deming's cycle) — walk through it in the simulator below.

持續品質改善（CQI）：由員工團隊找出並修復自己流程中的問題——持續進行，而非一次性專案。五個面向：流程焦點、顧客焦點、數據決策、員工賦權、全組織影響。醫療界最常用的配方是 FOCUS-PDCA（HCA 對 Shewhart／Deming 循環的擴充）——用下方模擬器走一遍。

Six Sigma is a data-driven method to eliminate variation, run as DMAIC: Define the scope and future state → Measure current performance → Analyze (often by flowcharting) → Improve toward the defined goals → Control so gains are permanent. Resource-intensive: serious training, dedicated time, long-term management commitment.

六標準差是消除變異的數據方法，依 DMAIC 執行：定義範圍與未來狀態 → 測量現況 → 分析（常用流程圖）→ 改善朝目標推進 → 控制使成果不回彈。高資源需求：紮實訓練、專責時間、長期高層承諾。

Lean (Toyota Production System) hunts waste: overproduction (unneeded pre-op bloodwork), wasted motion (patients and staff moving inefficiently), overprocessing (confusing duplicate bills), defects (mistakes and waiting), underutilized staff (NPs and PAs kept below their license). Six principles: continuous improvement attitude, value creation, unity of purpose, respect for the people who do the work, visualization, flexible regimentation.

精實（豐田生產系統）獵殺浪費：過度生產（不必要的術前抽血）、移動浪費（病人與人員低效移動）、過度處理（令人困惑的重複帳單）、缺陷（錯誤與等待）、人力低度運用（NP／PA 被用在證照以下的工作）。六原則：持續改善的態度、創造價值、目標一致、尊重做事的人、可視化、彈性紀律。
