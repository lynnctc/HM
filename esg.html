ESG in Healthcare

醫療機構的永續經營

Hospitals exist to heal people — yet health care itself produces roughly 4–5% of global greenhouse-gas emissions, runs some of the most energy-hungry buildings in any city, and employs a workforce burning out at record rates. ESG — Environmental, Social, Governance — is the framework investors, regulators and accreditors now use to ask: is this organization sustainable in every sense? This unit turns each letter into hospital management practice.

醫院的存在是為了治癒人——但醫療產業本身卻貢獻了全球約 4–5% 的溫室氣體排放、經營著城市裡最耗能的建築之一，員工倦怠率還屢創新高。ESG——環境、社會、治理——正是投資人、監理機關與評鑑機構用來追問「這個組織在每個意義上都永續嗎？」的框架。本單元把三個字母逐一變成醫院管理實務。

§1

Why a Hospital Cannot Ignore ESG

醫院為什麼躲不掉 ESG

ESG evaluates an organization on three non-financial dimensions: Environmental (emissions, energy, waste, water), Social (workforce, patients, community, human rights), and Governance (board oversight, ethics, transparency, accountability). It grew out of responsible investing, but for hospitals the logic is sharper than for most industries: climate change is a health threat — heat waves, dengue expansion, air pollution all land in your ER — so a hospital that damages the environment is quietly manufacturing its own future patients.

ESG 從三個非財務面向評估組織：環境（排放、能源、廢棄物、水）、社會（員工、病人、社區、人權）、治理（董事會監督、倫理、透明、當責）。它源自責任投資，但對醫院而言邏輯比多數產業更尖銳：氣候變遷本身就是健康威脅——熱浪、登革熱北擴、空污最後都送進你的急診——傷害環境的醫院，等於在悄悄製造自己未來的病人。

The scale of the problem 問題的規模

  • Health care ≈ 4.4% of global net emissions — if it were a country, the fifth-largest emitter (Health Care Without Harm, 2019) 醫療業約佔全球淨排放 4.4%——若是國家，排名第五
  • Hospitals run 24/7: energy intensity among the highest of all building types 醫院全年無休，能源強度居各類建築之冠

The pressure sources 壓力來源

  • Regulators: Taiwan's Climate Change Response Act (2023) writes net-zero 2050 into law 氣候變遷因應法把 2050 淨零入法
  • Systems moving first: England's NHS committed to net zero (2020) — the first national health system 英國 NHS 率先承諾淨零
  • Accreditors, insurers, corporate group owners, and talent — young staff increasingly choose employers by values 評鑑、保險人、集團母公司與人才市場

Materiality 重大性

  • You cannot do everything: identify the ESG issues most material to your organization and stakeholders, then commit resources there 先辨識對機構與利害關係人最重大的議題，再投入資源
  • This is U1's planning function wearing a new hat 這正是 U1 規劃功能的新應用

E — Where a Hospital's Footprint Hides

E：醫院的碳足跡藏在哪裡

Emissions are counted in three scopes: Scope 1 — what you burn on site (boilers, ambulances, anesthetic gases); Scope 2 — the electricity you buy; Scope 3 — everything upstream and downstream (pharmaceuticals, devices, food, staff commuting, waste). §3

S — The Social Ledger You Already Keep

S：你其實已經在記的社會帳

Here is the good news: most of the S in a hospital's ESG is material you already met in this course — ESG just forces you to measure and disclose it. Workforce: burnout, turnover, retention levers (U3, U6), workplace-violence protection, pay equity, DEI. The assigned reading on green HRM makes the link explicit: HR practices (green training, green performance appraisal) drive sustainability outcomes through employee behavior. Patients: safety culture and never events (U6), quality (U8). Community: health equity and determinants of health (U7) — a hospital's community-benefit programs are its most visible S.

好消息：醫院 ESG 的 S 大多是你在這門課已經遇過的內容——ESG 只是逼你測量並揭露它。員工：倦怠、離職、留任槓桿（U3、U6）、職場暴力防治、薪酬公平、多元共融。指定閱讀的綠色人力資源管理正是把這條線接明白：人資實務（綠色訓練、綠色績效考核）透過員工行為驅動永續成果。病人：安全文化與 never events（U6）、品質（U8）。社區：健康公平與健康決定因素（U7）——醫院的社區健康方案是最看得見的 S。

Manager's insight
管理者洞見 S is where ESG stops being a report and becomes retention strategy: hospitals that measurably invest in staff well-being recruit better in a nursing shortage. Your U3 motivation toolkit is literally an ESG instrument. S 是 ESG 從「報告書」變成「留才策略」的地方：在護理荒中，可被驗證投資員工福祉的醫院招募力就是更強。你在 U3 學的激勵工具箱，如假包換是一件 ESG 工具。
§4

G — Governance Makes E and S Stick

G：治理讓 E 與 S 不淪為口號

Without G, the rest is greenwashing. Governance for hospital ESG means: board-level oversight — a sustainability committee with authority and budget (recall U6: the governing board is legally responsible for the hospital's conduct); transparent reporting — Taiwan's FSC requires listed companies to publish sustainability reports, and large foundation-run hospitals increasingly publish theirs voluntarily; ethics infrastructure — COI disclosure (U6), anti-corruption, whistle-blower channels; and an ESG strategy map — the assigned reading's processing map shows how to cascade board commitments into department KPIs. Data governance belongs here too: the strategic-management reading argues big-data capabilities are what turn ESG intent into measurable performance — connecting straight to U8's “you can't manage what you can't measure.”

沒有 G，其他都是漂綠。醫院 ESG 的治理包括：董事會層級的監督——有權限有預算的永續委員會（回想 U6：理事會對醫院行為負法律責任）；透明報告——金管會要求上市櫃公司出永續報告書，大型財團法人醫院也漸多自願發布；倫理基礎建設——利益衝突申報（U6）、反貪腐、吹哨管道；以及ESG 策略地圖——指定閱讀的流程地圖示範如何把董事會承諾層層落到部門 KPI。資料治理也屬於 G：策略管理那篇指出，大數據能力是把 ESG 意圖變成可測量績效的關鍵——直接接回 U8 的「無法測量就無法管理」。

Taiwan Spotlight 台灣視角

The policy stack: Taiwan legislated net-zero 2050 in the Climate Change Response Act (Feb 2023); the National Development Council's 2050 pathway names buildings and energy among its transitions — hospitals sit squarely in both. MOHW and the healthcare sector have followed with hospital energy-saving and smart-hospital initiatives, and medical centers have begun setting up sustainability committees and joining net-zero pledges. Discussion: NHI global budgets (U9) don't yet pay for decarbonization — who should fund a hospital's net-zero transition: the payer, the government, or the hospital's own margin?

政策疊層：《氣候變遷因應法》（2023 年 2 月）把 2050 淨零入法；國發會 2050 路徑圖點名建築與能源轉型——醫院正好兩者都沾。衛福部與醫界隨之推動醫院節能與智慧醫院方案，醫學中心也開始成立永續委員會、加入淨零倡議。討論：健保總額（U9）目前不給付減碳成本——醫院的淨零轉型該由誰買單：保險人、政府，還是醫院自己的結餘？
