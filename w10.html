Ethics & Law

倫理與法律

Legal and ethical are not synonyms: an act can be legal yet wrong, or ethical yet forbidden. Managers live in that overlap daily — consent forms, resource allocation, end-of-life policies. This week: the four principles that structure every bioethics argument, who owes what to whom in the provider–patient relationship, and Taiwan's own answers at the edges of life.

合法與合乎倫理不是同義詞：一件事可能合法卻不對，也可能合乎倫理卻被禁止。管理者天天活在這個交疊地帶——同意書、資源分配、臨終政策。本週內容：支撐所有生命倫理論證的四大原則、醫病關係中誰對誰負什麼責任，以及台灣在生命兩端給出的自己的答案。

§1

Legal? Ethical? Both? Neither?

合法？合倫理？都是？都不是？

Law governs relationships among people, organizations and government — via legislation, regulation, and common law (judge-made law built on precedent). Ethics stems from family, community, and professional traditions; violating it may cost your license or your standing rather than your liberty. Malpractice — a professional's improper conduct through intent, carelessness or ignorance — can be civil (tort) or, with reckless disregard for safety, criminal. Sort each act into the 2×2:

法律規範人、組織與政府間的關係——透過立法、命令與普通法（以判例累積的法官造法）。倫理源自家庭、社群與專業傳統；違反的代價通常是執照或聲譽，而非自由。業務過失（malpractice）——專業人員因故意、疏忽或無知的不當行為——可為民事（侵權），若「罔顧他人安全」則屬刑事。把下列行為分進 2×2：

§2

The Four Principles — Case Analyzer

四大原則個案分析器

Respect for persons / Autonomy 尊重自主

  • The patient controls his or her own body; persuade, never coerce. Includes truth-telling, confidentiality, fidelity病人對自己的身體有決定權；可建議、不可強迫。含誠實告知、保密、守信

Beneficence 行善

  • Do all you can to benefit this patient in this situation在此情境為此病人謀取最大利益

Non-maleficence 不傷害

  • “Do no harm” — to the patient, others, or society「勿傷害」——對病人、他人與社會

Justice 正義

  • Fairness in burdens, benefits, and scarce resources; uphold the law負擔、利益與稀缺資源的公平分配；遵守法律

§3

Who Owes What to Whom

誰對誰負什麼責任

Provider responsibilities 提供者責任

  • EMTALA: emergency treatment regardless of ability to pay 緊急醫療不問支付能力
  • Licensure & malpractice law 執照與業務過失法
  • Fiduciary duty — obligation to those who placed trust in you 受託義務

Provider rights 提供者權利

  • Fairness and dignity from employers 受公平與尊嚴對待
  • Protection from harassment 免受騷擾
  • Generally, excusal from care they disagree with (under litigation) 原則上可迴避良心牴觸之照護（爭訟中）

Patient rights 病人權利

  • Self-determination 自主決定
  • Confidentiality 資訊保密
  • Informed consent or competent surrogate 知情同意或適格代理人
  • Right to refuse care 拒絕醫療
  • Emergency treatment 緊急救治

Patient responsibilities 病人責任

  • Ask questions 提問
  • Provide accurate information 提供正確資訊
  • Attempt to follow directions and prescriptions 盡力遵循醫囑
§4

The Edges of Life — Taiwan's Answers

生命的兩端——台灣的答案

Beginning of life: managers must run policies on contraception and abortion consistent with mission, values — and law. Taiwan's 優生保健法 (Genetic Health Act) Art. 9 permits induced abortion on the pregnant woman's decision under listed conditions: genetic/infectious/psychiatric disease in a spouse, hereditary disease within four degrees of kinship, medical risk to life or health, likely fetal malformation, pregnancy from rape or legally barred relations, or impact on mental health or family life.

生命起點：管理者執行的避孕與人工流產政策，必須同時符合使命、價值——與法律。台灣《優生保健法》第 9 條列舉得依孕婦自願施行人工流產的情形：本人或配偶患礙優生之遺傳、傳染或精神疾病；四親等內血親有礙優生之遺傳疾病；懷孕或分娩有生命或身心健康風險；胎兒有畸形發育之虞；因強制性交或依法不得結婚者相姦受孕；以及懷孕或生產將影響心理健康或家庭生活。

End of life: two laws side by side 臨終：兩部法律對照

Scope: terminal patients only. Document: a hospice/life-sustaining choice consent, signable by the patient or family. May decline: CPR and life-sustaining treatment; palliative care is provided. No prior consultation required — the form can simply be signed.

適用：僅限末期病人。文件：預立安寧緩和醫療暨維生醫療抉擇意願書，本人或家屬皆可簽。可拒絕：心肺復甦術與維生醫療；並提供安寧緩和照護。不需事前諮商——填寫即可。

Scope (5 conditions): terminal illness, irreversible coma, permanent vegetative state, severe dementia, plus other announced conditions. Document: an Advance Decision (AD), signable only by the person, with mental capacity, after a mandatory advance care planning (ACP) consultation; a health care agent may be appointed. May decline: life-sustaining treatment (incl. CPR) and artificial nutrition/hydration; palliative care becomes a mandatory offering.

適用（五款）：末期、不可逆昏迷、永久植物人、極重度失智，及其他公告重症。文件：預立醫療決定（AD），僅限本人、心智能力健全者，且須先經預立醫療照護諮商（ACP）；可指定醫療委任代理人。可拒絕：維持生命治療（含 CPR）與人工營養及流體餵養；緩和醫療改為必要提供。

Manager's angle
管理者視角 Your hospital must build the ACP clinic, train consultation teams, register ADs, and write escalation policies for when families disagree with a valid AD. Ethics is an operations problem too. 醫院要開設 ACP 門診、訓練諮商團隊、註記預立醫療決定，並訂定「家屬與有效 AD 意見相左」時的處理政策。倫理同時也是營運問題。
§5

IRB & Why Bother

IRB 與「為何要認真」

The Institutional Review Board (IRB) reviews research involving human subjects before it starts — protecting autonomy (informed consent), weighing benefit against harm, and watching justice in subject selection: the four principles running as a standing committee. And the textbook's practical reasons to take ethics seriously: resolve disputes, keep a clear conscience, keep patients' and colleagues' respect, stay efficient, reduce burnout.

研究倫理審查委員會（IRB）在人體研究開始前審查——保護自主（知情同意）、衡量利益與傷害、把關受試者選擇的正義：四大原則以常設委員會的形式運轉。教科書給的務實理由：化解糾紛、保持良心清明、贏得病人與同儕敬重、維持效率、減少倦怠。
