Six Case Studies

個案討論：書面報告＋口頭發表

The heart of this course's assessment: in groups, own one of six frontier topics in healthcare management, write a research paper around a conceptual framework, and teach it to the class in a 40–60 minute session where every member takes the stage.

本課評量的核心：分組認領六大前沿主題之一，以概念架構為軸寫成研究報告，再用 40–60 分鐘把它「教」給全班——每位組員都要上台。

Paper due 書面截止: the week BEFORE your presentation week, by COB 發表前一週當日 COB 前 · Language 語言: English or Mandarin 中英皆可 · Late work not accepted without prior agreement 未事先同意不收遲交

Topics

六大主題（每組認領一題）

① Patient safety 病人安全

  • The Safety of Inpatient Health Care
  • Global patient safety action plan 2021–2030
  • Connects to 對應單元: W6、W8

② Blockchain in healthcare 區塊鏈

  • Can blockchain improve healthcare management?
  • EHR management, medical-supply tracking; security, privacy & outcomes reviews

③ Telemedicine 遠距醫療

④ Big data in healthcare 大數據

  • Data analytics for decision-making; patient-centric approaches

  • Connects to: W8 §5；銜接醫療大數據課程

⑤ Smart healthcare 智慧醫療

  • Change-management models in healthcare; adoption challenges

  • Connects to: W1 §5 變革管理、W11

⑥ Digital transformation 數位轉型

  • State of research; EHR → clinical management systems

  • Connects to: W1、W11

Case briefs — how to attack each topic 個案鷹架：每題怎麼打

Each brief gives you the battlefield map: what the topic is really about, the diagnostic angles that score well, and the trap most groups fall into. Expand yours — then read the others anyway, because you must question them in class.

每份鷹架給你戰場地圖：這題真正在談什麼、哪些診斷切角能拿分、多數組會踩的陷阱。展開你那題——其他題也要讀，因為課堂上你得對他們提問。

What it's really about: two decades after To Err is Human, inpatient harm is still common — the question has shifted from “are errors happening?” to “why don't safety systems stick?” Angles that score: safety culture vs blame culture (W6), RCA quality, WHO's Global Patient Safety Action Plan 2021–2030 as a management framework, measurement problems (W8: are we counting harm or counting reports?). Trap: retelling famous error stories without a management diagnosis — the paper must analyze the system, not the incident.

真正在談：《To Err is Human》二十多年後，住院傷害依然常見——問題已從「有沒有錯誤」變成「安全系統為何扎不了根」。得分切角：安全文化 vs 究責文化（W6）、RCA 品質、WHO 全球病安行動計畫 2021–2030 作為管理框架、測量問題（W8：我們數的是傷害還是通報？）。陷阱：只複述知名醫療事故而沒有管理診斷——報告要分析的是「系統」，不是「事件」。

What it's really about: a decade of pilots in EHR access control, supply-chain tracking and consent management — and strikingly few production deployments. Angles that score: treat it as a technology-adoption case: what problem does blockchain solve that a normal database with good governance doesn't? Compare hype-cycle position vs GenAI; analyze why interoperability and incentive problems (W9) blocked adoption. A critical verdict, well argued, beats cheerleading. Trap: explaining how blockchain works for five pages — this is a management course, not a cryptography course.

真正在談：十年來在病歷存取控制、供應鏈追蹤與同意管理的試點很多——真正上線的極少。得分切角：把它當「科技導入」個案：區塊鏈解決了什麼「治理良好的普通資料庫」解決不了的問題？對照它與 GenAI 在炒作週期的位置；分析互通性與誘因問題（W9）為何卡死落地。有論證的批判結論勝過啦啦隊。陷阱：花五頁解釋區塊鏈原理——這是管理課，不是密碼學課。

What it's really about: COVID-19 forced the natural experiment; the management question is what survives peacetime — reimbursement, licensure, and which visit types genuinely work remotely. Angles that score: Taiwan's 通訊診察治療辦法 (revised 2024) as the local regulatory frame (W9/W11); payment design — does FFS pay for tele-visits fairly (W9)?; equity — telehealth can widen or narrow access (W7); quality evidence by specialty. Trap: “telemedicine is convenient” without confronting who pays and who gets left behind.

真正在談：COVID-19 逼出了自然實驗；管理問題是「承平時期什麼留得下來」——給付、執業規範、哪些診別真的適合遠距。得分切角：以通訊診察治療辦法（2024 修正）為在地法規框架（W9/W11）；支付設計——論量計酬對遠距是否公平（W9）；公平性——遠距可能擴大也可能縮小可近性（W7）；各專科的品質實證。陷阱：只說「遠距很方便」，不敢面對誰買單、誰被落下。

What it's really about: most hospitals drown in data and starve for decisions — the constraint is management capability, not storage. Angles that score: people analytics for retention (W3), quality dashboards and SPC (W8), Taiwan's NHIRD as a world-class research asset with governance controversies (opt-out debates), the digital-competence gap between managers and professionals (assigned reading). Trap: listing V's (volume, velocity…) — examiners have read that paragraph a hundred times.

真正在談：多數醫院淹死在資料裡、卻餓死在決策上——瓶頸是管理能力，不是儲存空間。得分切角：用於留任的人力分析（W3）、品質儀表板與 SPC（W8）、台灣健保資料庫作為世界級研究資產及其治理爭議（退出權辯論）、管理者與專業人員的數位能力落差（指定閱讀）。陷阱：羅列幾個 V（volume、velocity…）——評分者已看過一百次那一段。

What it's really about: not gadgets — change management (W1's four steps) applied to clinical workflows: smart wards, AI triage, IoT monitoring. Angles that score: pick ONE smart-hospital implementation and analyze adoption barriers with a change-management model (assigned reading does exactly this); staff resistance as a motivation problem (W3); TFDA registration boundaries for AI components (W11). Trap: a catalog of shiny technologies with no organization in sight.

真正在談：不是酷炫裝置——是把變革管理（W1 四步驟）套用到臨床流程：智慧病房、AI 檢傷、IoT 監測。得分切角：挑「一個」智慧醫院導入案，用變革管理模型分析採用障礙（指定閱讀正是這麼做）；把員工抗拒當成動機問題（W3）；AI 元件的 TFDA 查驗登記界線（W11）。陷阱：目錄式羅列閃亮科技，卻看不到任何組織。

What it's really about: the umbrella case — EHR-to-platform evolution, value creation, and the critical view (assigned reading: “for better or worse”). Angles that score: define transformation vs digitization; who captures the value — hospital, vendor, or payer?; governance of transformation programs (W6 board oversight; G in ESG); burnout cost of bad EHR design as an S issue. Trap: treating “more IT” as automatically “transformed” — the critical reading exists precisely to punish that.

真正在談：總傘型題目——從病歷電子化到平台化的演進、價值創造，以及批判視角（指定閱讀：for better or worse）。得分切角：先區分「轉型」與「數位化」；價值被誰拿走——醫院、廠商還是付款方？；轉型計畫的治理（W6 董事會監督；ESG 的 G）；爛系統造成的倦怠是 S 議題。陷阱：把「更多資訊系統」直接當「轉型成功」——那篇批判文獻的存在就是為了懲罰這種寫法。

主題健檢建議 Instructor's note for the next revision: the blockchain topic's academic and industry momentum has clearly cooled since these cases were set. Candidate replacements that fit this course tightly: “Deploying GenAI in a hospital” (extends W11 into a management case) or “Healthcare cybersecurity” (ransomware as a patient-safety and governance case, natural fit for an IM department). Groups may also be offered the swap as an option while keeping blockchain available. 給下次改版的授課者備註：區塊鏈主題的學術與產業熱度自出題以來已明顯降溫。貼合本課的候選替代題：「醫院導入 GenAI」（把 W11 延伸成管理個案）或「醫療資安」（勒索軟體＝病安＋治理個案，與資管系定位吻合）。也可保留區塊鏈、同時開放各組選擇替代題。
2

The Written Paper (25%)

書面報告規格

Length & format 篇幅與格式

  • 5–6 pages (English) or 7–8 pages (Mandarin), single-spaced 英文 5–6 頁或中文 7–8 頁，單行距
  • Cover page & references not counted; charts, graphs, photos, lists and extended quotes don't count against page limits 封面與參考文獻不計頁；圖表照片清單引文不佔頁數
  • Page numbers on everything 全文加頁碼

Structure 結構

  • Introduction: your conceptual framework (1 page) + brief intro（≤1.5 pages）— what is this issue? why does it matter? what should we learn? 概念架構一頁＋簡介：談什麼、為何重要、該學到什麼
  • Main text 本文（recommended spine 建議主軸：diagnose → prescribe → stress-test，見 W12 §2）
  • Conclusion 結論
  • References: APA 7, ≥5 sources, primarily peer-reviewed journals; reputable web sources OK (gov agencies, professional organizations); Wikipedia is NOT reputable 至少 5 篇、以期刊為主；Wikipedia 不算
  • Tables/graphs immediately after references 圖表緊接參考文獻之後

Attachments 必附

  • Teamwork sheet 分工表: everyone has ≥1 substantive task — “summarizing” or “oral presentation” alone doesn't count 每人至少一項實質工作，僅「彙整」或「上台」不算
  • GenAI declaration AI 揭露聲明: state the tools used and how + attach transcripts 說明工具與用途，並附對話紀錄
3

The Oral Presentation (15%)

口頭發表規則

Format 形式

  • PowerPoint only（not PDF or Canva）僅限 PPT
  • Professional, readable design: margins, white space, templates 版面專業易讀
  • 40–60 minutes per group 每組 40–60 分鐘

Participation 參與

  • Every student presents ≥5 minutes 每人至少上台 5 分鐘
  • Other groups must raise ≥1 question 其他組至少提出一個問題
  • Inter- and intra-group peer grading 組間與組內互評

Tips from W12 發表要訣

  • Lead with the diagnosis, not the literature 先講診斷，不要從文獻堆開場
  • One measurable problem sentence 一句可測量的問題陳述
  • End on the stress-test: quality × money × ethics/law 用三重檢核收尾
Rubric
評分規準 Papers and frameworks are graded on the official 10/7/4/1 rubric — paper text 20%, integration & depth 20%, neutral voice 20%, synthesis 15%, conclusions 15% — see the Syllabus page §5. Similar considerations apply to class participation. 書面與概念架構依正式 10/7/4/1 rubric 評分——文本 20%、整合深度 20%、中立觀點 20%、綜整 15%、結論 15%——詳見大綱頁 §5；課堂參與比照辦理。
