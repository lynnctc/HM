Management Case Analysis

期末專題：管理個案分析

One organization, one problem, one defensible prescription. Use the whole course: diagnose with theory, prescribe with feasibility, stress-test with quality, money, ethics and law.

一個機構、一個問題、一個站得住腳的處方。用上整門課：以理論診斷、以可行性開方、以品質、財務、倫理與法律做壓力測試。

Team 組隊: solo or pairs 個人或兩人 · Weight 佔分: replaces Module 4 homework 取代模組四作業

1

Choosing a Case

選題

Any health care organization with a management problem: hospital, clinic chain, long-term-care facility, health bureau, health-tech company. Sources: teaching cases, public news reports, published case studies — or a fully fictional but realistic composite. Never use insider information or identifiable patient/staff data.

任何有管理問題的醫療組織皆可：醫院、連鎖診所、長照機構、衛生局、健康科技公司。來源：教學個案、公開新聞、已發表個案研究——或完全虛構但合理的綜合個案。嚴禁使用內部消息或可識別的病人／員工資料。

Idea starters 題目靈感

  • ER overcrowding at a regional hospital 區域醫院急診壅塞
  • Nursing turnover in a post-acute ward 亞急性病房護理離職潮
  • Rolling out an ambient AI scribe safely 安全導入 AI 臨床紀錄
  • A clinic chain absorbing the copay reform 診所連鎖因應部分負擔新制
  • Merging two hospital pharmacies 兩院藥局整併

Timeline 時程

  • W10: ½-page pitch (problem + org + framework guess) 半頁選題說明
  • W11: instructor feedback 教師回饋
  • W12: 10-min talk + 3-min Q&A; 3-page report due 17:00 發表＋書面

Required structure 必要結構

  • Diagnose 診斷: problem in one measurable sentence + ≥2 course frameworks
  • Prescribe 處方: one intervention, steps, metric, check date
  • Stress-test 壓力測試: quality × money × ethics/law
2

Grading Rubric

評分規準

Criterion 項目Weight 佔比What excellent looks like 優等的樣子
Diagnosis & theory use 問題診斷與理論運用30%Root causes separated from symptoms; ≥2 frameworks used correctly and named by week 症狀與根因分離；正確使用並標註至少兩個課程框架
Feasibility of solution 方案可行性30%Costs, resisters and incentives addressed; realistic steps; measurable metric with a check date 成本、抗拒者與誘因都被處理；步驟務實；指標可測且有查核日
Quality / ethics / legal check 三重檢核20%Donabedian element identified; payment-system compatibility argued; four-principles & statute check done 指出動到的 Donabedian 要素；論證支付相容性；完成四原則與法規檢核
Presentation 口頭發表20%Clear story in 10 minutes; direct answers in Q&A; readable slides 十分鐘說清楚；答辯切題；投影片易讀
Ground rules
基本規則 Public or fictional cases only · cite every borrowed fact · disclose AI assistance in a footnote (what tool, for what) · both members present if a pair. 僅可用公開或虛構個案・借用的事實皆註明出處・以註腳揭露 AI 使用（什麼工具、用在哪）・兩人組須皆上台。
