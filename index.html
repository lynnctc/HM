Management for Healthcare Organizations

醫務管理

How do you lead a hospital, motivate a burned-out nursing team, fix a broken discharge process, read Taiwan's NHI payment system, and decide whether ChatGPT belongs in the clinic? Twelve weeks through the management of health care organizations — with classification games, case simulators, a cost-sharing calculator, and self-check quizzes in every unit.

如何領導一家醫院、激勵倦怠的護理團隊、改善卡住的出院流程、看懂台灣健保支付制度，並判斷 ChatGPT 能不能進臨床？十二週走完醫療機構管理——每個單元都有分類遊戲、個案模擬器、部分負擔計算機與自我測驗。

Instructor 授課：Yu-Hsiu Lin, Ph.D. 林育秀 3 hr / week（Mon 2h + Wed 1h on-line） Bilingual EN + ZH 教材中英並行 Texts: Buchbinder & Shanks · Healey · Gupta · Kiel

Case Study (40%)

個案討論：六大主題

The main graded work: in groups, take one of six assigned topics, write a research paper (due the week before your slot), and present it in the case-study weeks — 40–60 minutes per group, every member on stage.

最主要的正式評量：分組認領六大主題之一，完成研究報告（發表前一週繳交書面），並於個案週上台發表——每組 40–60 分鐘，每位成員都要上台。

Six topics 六大主題

  • Patient safety 病人安全 · Blockchain 區塊鏈
  • Telemedicine 遠距醫療 · Big data 大數據
  • Smart healthcare 智慧醫療 · Digital transformation 數位轉型

Weights 配分

  • Written paper 書面 25%5–6 頁英文或 7–8 頁中文、APA 7、含概念架構
  • Oral presentation 口頭 15%PPT、每人至少 5 分鐘、他組提問

Must-haves 必附

  • Teamwork sheet 分工表
  • GenAI declaration + transcripts AI 揭露＋對話紀錄
  • Full spec 完整規格：Case Study page 個案頁

Course Rules & Resources

Academic integrity 學術倫理

  • Submissions checked via SafeAssignment 繳交物經 SafeAssignment 查核
  • Cite everything you borrow; “I didn't mean to” is still plagiarism 引用皆須註明，「不是故意的」仍是抄襲
  • Disclose GenAI tools and prompts 揭露 AI 工具與 prompts
  • Late work not accepted without prior agreement 未事先同意不收遲交

How to use this site 網站使用

  • Each unit ≈ one lecture topic; calendar mapping on the Syllabus page 單元×行事曆對照見大綱頁
  • Play every interactive at least once 每個互動元件至少玩一次
  • Quizzes & practices are ungraded — retry freely 測驗與練習不計分，可重複作答
