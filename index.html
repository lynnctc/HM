Management for Healthcare Organizations

醫務管理

How do you lead a hospital, motivate a burned-out nursing team, fix a broken discharge process, read Taiwan's NHI payment system, and decide whether ChatGPT belongs in the clinic? Twelve weeks through the management of health care organizations — with classification games, case simulators, a cost-sharing calculator, and self-check quizzes in every unit.

如何領導一家醫院、激勵倦怠的護理團隊、改善卡住的出院流程、看懂台灣健保支付制度，並判斷 ChatGPT 能不能進臨床？十二週走完醫療機構管理——每個單元都有分類遊戲、個案模擬器、部分負擔計算機與自我測驗。

Term Project (Module 4)

期末專題：管理個案分析

Pick a real (public) or teaching case of a health care organization in trouble — then diagnose it with course frameworks, prescribe a management solution, and stress-test it against quality, ethics and law. Topic opens at Week 10; presentations at Week 12.

選一個真實（公開報導）或教學用的醫療機構管理個案——用課程框架診斷問題、開出管理處方，再以品質、倫理與法規做壓力測試。第 10 週開題，第 12 週發表。

Deliverables 交付

  • ½-page case pitch (W10) 半頁選題說明
  • 3-page written analysis 三頁書面分析
  • 10-minute presentation (W12) 十分鐘發表

Grading rubric 評分

  • Diagnosis & theory use 30%問題診斷與理論運用
  • Feasibility of solution 30%方案可行性
  • Quality / ethics / legal check 20%品質倫理法規檢核
  • Presentation 20%口頭發表

Ground rules 基本規則

  • Public or fictional cases only — no insider or identifiable patient data 僅可用公開或虛構個案，不得含個資
  • Solo or teams of two 個人或兩人一組
  • Details 詳見 Project page 專題頁

Course Rules & Resources

修課規則與資源

Textbooks 教科書

  • Buchbinder & Shanks, Introduction to Health Care Management (Jones & Bartlett)
  • Healey, Introduction to Health Care Services (Jossey-Bass)
  • 黃維民《衛生行政與健康保險》（華杏）— NHI 週參考

Academic integrity 學術倫理

  • Honor code applies to every submission 榮譽守則適用所有繳交物
  • Cite everything you borrow; “I didn't mean to” is still plagiarism 引用皆須註明，「不是故意的」仍是抄襲
  • Disclose AI assistance in a footnote 以註腳揭露 AI 使用方式

How to use this site 網站使用

  • Each week ≈ one 3-hour session 每頁對應一週三小時
  • Play every interactive at least once 每個互動元件至少玩一次
  • Quizzes are ungraded — retry freely 測驗不計分，可重複作答
