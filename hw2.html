Taiwan Health System Dossier

台灣醫療體系小檔案

Build a compact, sourced dossier of the system you will one day manage: its people, one hospital's governance, and one public-health program — all with current official numbers.

為你未來要管理的體系做一份精簡、有出處的小檔案：它的人力、一家醫院的治理，與一項公衛方案——全部使用最新官方數據。

Ungraded practice 不計分: self-study rehearsal — the dossier skills (sourcing official statistics, citing statutes) are exactly what the graded case-study paper requires. 不計分排練——查官方統計、引用法條的功夫，正是個案書面報告需要的。

Tasks

題目（自評配分 100）

Part 1 · Workforce table 醫事人力對照表（40 分）

Pick three licensed professions (e.g., 醫師、護理師、醫事放射師、物理治療師、公共衛生師). For each: education path and national exam, legal scope of practice (cite the statute), current practicing headcount (MOHW statistics, cite year), and one workforce challenge. Present as a table + 3 short notes.

挑三種法定職類（如醫師、護理師、醫事放射師、物理治療師、公共衛生師）。每種列出：養成路徑與國考、法定業務範圍（引用法條）、現行執業人數（衛福部統計，註明年度）、一項人力挑戰。以表格＋三段短評呈現。

Part 2 · Governance snapshot 治理快照（30 分）

For one Taiwanese hospital (may reuse HW1's), map its governance: board (董事會), superintendent (院長), key C-suite roles, and where nursing sits. Compare with the U.S. textbook structure (board–MEC–CEO): name two similarities and two differences.

為一家台灣醫院（可沿用 HW1）描繪治理結構：董事會、院長、關鍵高階主管、護理部的位置。與美國教科書結構（理事會–MEC–CEO）比較：寫出兩個相似、兩個相異之處。

Part 3 · Public health in action 公衛實戰（30 分）

Choose one Taiwanese public-health program (e.g., 四癌篩檢、疫苗接種、登革熱防治、菸害防制). Analyze it through the three core functions — assessment, policy development, assurance — one short paragraph each, and add one equity observation: who might this program still miss?

選一項台灣公衛方案（如四癌篩檢、疫苗接種、登革熱防治、菸害防制）。以三大核心功能——評估、政策發展、確保——各寫一段分析，並加一段公平性觀察：這個方案可能還漏掉誰？

Format 格式 3–4 pages PDF. Every number needs a source and year; MOHW statistical portal (dep.mohw.gov.tw) is your friend. 3–4 頁 PDF。每個數字都要有來源與年度；衛福部統計處網站是你的好朋友。
