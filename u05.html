Health Care Providers

醫事人力

A manager who cannot tell an NP from a PA, or an RN from an LPN, will mis-staff a clinic and maybe break the law. In this unit we map the professions that deliver care — who trains how long, who may do what, and who reports to whom — because every staffing, budgeting and quality decision you will ever make sits on top of this map.

分不清 NP 與 PA、RN 與 LPN 的管理者，會把診所人力配錯、甚至觸法。本單元畫出提供照護的職類地圖——誰受多久訓練、誰能做什麼、誰向誰報告——因為你未來每個人力、預算與品質決策，都建立在這張地圖上。

§1

Primary Care: the Front Door

基層醫療：體系的前門

The primary care provider is the cornerstone of most health systems — the first point of contact, the generalist who diagnoses, treats, prevents, counsels on diet and hygiene, and refers to specialists. The team includes primary care physicians, physician assistants, and nurse practitioners.

基層醫療提供者是多數醫療體系的基石——第一線接觸點，全科通才：診斷、治療、預防、給飲食衛教，並轉診給專科。團隊成員包括基層醫師、醫師助理與專科護理師。

MD vs DO 兩種醫師

  • MD — allopathic physician 對抗療法醫師
  • DO — osteopathic: adds emphasis on the musculoskeletal system, preventive and holistic care 骨療醫學：強調肌肉骨骼系統、預防與全人照護
  • Both fully licensed with the same treatment authority 兩者皆完整執照、治療權限相同

PA 醫師助理

  • Practices under supervision of an MD/DO 在醫師督導下執業
  • Histories, physicals, routine labs, minor injuries 病史、理學檢查、常規檢驗、小外傷
  • Bachelor's/master's + state boards 學碩士＋州考

NP 專科護理師

  • Advanced-practice nurse; overlapping duties with PA 進階護理；職務與 PA 高度重疊
  • Greater autonomy: may practice independently and prescribe 自主性更高：可獨立執業與開立處方（依州規定）
§2

The Nursing Ladder

護理層級

Nursing is the largest slice of the health workforce, and it comes in levels. Managers must know exactly where assessment and delegation stop on the way down.

護理是醫療人力中最大的一塊，而且分層級。管理者必須清楚知道：往下走到哪一層，「評估」與「授權他人」的權限就停止了。

Level 層級Core duties 核心職務Boundary 界線
RN Registered Nurse 註冊護理師Treat, coordinate care, educate patients & public 治療、協調照護、衛教Assesses, plans, delegates 可評估、計畫、授權
LPN Licensed Practical Nurse 執業護士Bedside basics under MD/RN direction: vitals, injections, wound dressing, feeding, transfers 在醫師／RN 指示下的床邊照護Works under direction 受指示執業
CNA Certified Nursing Assistant 照顧服務員Call lights, vitals, bathing, beds, feeding, ambulation 生活照顧與基本測量Does not assess, interpret, decide, or delegate 不可評估、判讀、決策、授權
Taiwan mapping
台灣對照 Taiwan's ladder differs: 護理師 (professional nurse, university-level licensure) and 護士 (nurse, vocational-level; new licenses discontinued) — plus 專科護理師 (NP, requiring RN experience + training + national certification). CNAs map loosely to 照顧服務員, central to long-term care. 台灣層級不同：護理師（大學層級國考）與護士（專科層級，已停發新照）——另有專科護理師（需護理師資歷＋訓練＋國家甄審）。CNA 約略對應照顧服務員，是長照主力。
§3

Game: Who Can Do This?

遊戲：這件事誰能做？

A task lands on the ward. Pick the lowest-level provider who may legally perform it (per the U.S. textbook definitions above). Staffing to the lowest safe level is a core managerial cost skill.

病房來了一項任務。選出依法可執行它的最低層級人員（依上方美國教科書定義）。把工作配到「最低而安全」的層級，是管理者的核心成本技能。

§4

The Rest of the Roster

其他職類點名

Dentists diagnose and treat diseases of teeth, mouth and jaws (specialties: periodontics 牙周, prosthodontics 修復, endodontics 牙髓). Dental hygienists go beyond cleaning — oral disease assessment, sometimes X-rays, fluorides, even local anesthesia depending on state law. Dental assistants run the office and chairside logistics but cannot clean teeth.

牙醫師診治牙齒口腔頜面疾病（專科：牙周、修復、牙髓）。牙科衛生師不只洗牙——口腔疾病評估、部分州可照 X 光、塗氟、甚至局部麻醉。牙科助理負責診間行政與跟診，不可洗牙。

Psychiatrists are physicians (can prescribe; subspecialties from addiction to sleep). Psychologists hold doctorates (PhD/EdD/PsyD) + supervised experience + license. Psychiatric nursing runs its own ladder (RN-PMH → APRN-PMH → PMH-NP). Social workers coordinate programs and services to improve quality of life.

精神科醫師是醫師（可開藥；次專科從成癮到睡眠）。心理師持博士學位＋督導經驗＋執照（台灣為臨床／諮商心理師，碩士層級國考）。精神科護理有自己的階梯。社工師統籌方案與資源、改善生活品質。

Four to know: radiologic technicians (take radiographs), diagnostic medical sonographers (ultrasound), nuclear medicine technologists (administer radiopharmaceuticals), radiation therapists (deliver linear-accelerator treatments with curative intent on the oncology team).

四種要認得：放射技術員（照 X 光）、超音波檢查師、核醫技術師（施打放射性藥劑）、放射治療師（在腫瘤團隊以直線加速器執行放療）。台灣統整為「醫事放射師」國考職類。

PT treats physical injuries; OT teaches daily-living skills for physical/emotional disabilities; athletic trainers prevent and treat musculoskeletal injuries; orthotists/prosthetists fit braces and artificial limbs; speech-language pathologists and audiologists handle communication and hearing; art/dance/music/horticultural therapists use creative media therapeutically.

物理治療師處理身體損傷；職能治療師訓練日常生活功能；運動防護員預防處理運動傷害；矯具義肢師製作矯具與義肢；語言治療師與聽力師負責溝通與聽力；藝術／舞蹈／音樂／園藝治療師以創作媒材治療。

§5

Workforce Trends — and Taiwan's Numbers

人力趨勢與台灣數字

Health care remains among the fastest-growing sectors of the U.S. economy, powered by technology, pharmaceuticals and the aging baby boom. But the jobs carry hazards — needle-sticks, back injuries, violence, fatigue — and chronic wage disputes. Teamwork and communication across all these professions is repeatedly identified as the critical defense against medical error.

醫療照護仍是美國成長最快的產業之一，動力來自科技、藥物與嬰兒潮老化。但這些工作伴隨職業危害——針扎、下背傷、職場暴力、疲勞——與長期的薪資爭議。跨職類的團隊合作與溝通，一再被證實是防止醫療錯誤的關鍵防線。

Taiwan Spotlight 台灣視角

Class exercise from the deck: how many practicing physicians and nurses does Taiwan have? Look up the MOHW statistical yearbook (衛福部統計處) for the current counts of practicing 醫師 and 護理人員, then compute per-1,000-population density and compare with OECD averages. Discussion: Taiwan trains enough nurses — but how many licensed nurses are actually practicing? What does that gap say to a hospital manager?

投影片的課堂練習：台灣有多少執業醫師與護理人員？請查衛福部統計處年報的執業醫師與護理人員數，換算每千人口密度並與 OECD 平均比較。討論：台灣護理養成人數足夠——但領照者有多少比例實際執業？這個落差對醫院管理者意味著什麼？
