Homework: the Conceptual Framework (15%)

作業：概念架構圖

A conceptual framework is a mental map of a topic: it captures the key elements, identifies causal and effect variables, and shows the relationships and their directions. Each student submits one framework during the semester, based on the assigned readings of a lecture chapter (not the case-study topics). Only five students per chapter may present — register your chapter with the TA early. Submit Word or PDF, file name 學號-姓名-Unit N, before that day's class; be prepared to defend it in class. GenAI may be used if you disclose the tool and prompts. After class all frameworks are shared as one PDF on e-Course so everyone can improve.

概念架構是主題的心智地圖：抓出關鍵元素、辨識因果變數、標出關係及其方向。每位學生整學期繳交一份，取材自講授章節（非個案主題）的指定閱讀。每章限 5 位發表——請儘早向助教登記章節。繳交 Word 或 PDF，檔名 學號-姓名-Unit N，於該單元上課前繳交，並準備在課堂答辯。可用生成式 AI，但須揭露工具與 prompts。課後全班架構圖將彙整為一份 PDF 上傳 e-Course，供互相觀摩改進。

Missed class?
缺課者 Prepare a full narrative paper explaining your framework with coverage of all assigned articles (with citations), and submit it together with the framework on the next class day. No page limit. 須另交一份完整的敘事說明：解釋你的架構、涵蓋所有指定文獻（附引用），於下次上課日連同架構圖繳交。無頁數限制。

§4

Case Study (40%)

個案討論：口頭 15%＋書面 25%

Six case-study topics run in the presentation weeks. Each group completes one research paper, submitted the week before your presentation week (COB), then presents in class.

個案報告共六大主題，於發表週輪流進行。各組完成一篇研究報告，於發表前一週（COB 前）繳交書面，再於課堂發表。

Six topics 六大主題

  • Patient safety 病人安全
  • Blockchain in healthcare 區塊鏈
  • Telemedicine 遠距醫療
  • Big data in healthcare 大數據
  • Smart healthcare 智慧醫療
  • Digital transformation 數位轉型

Paper 書面（25%）

  • 5–6 pages English or 7–8 pages Mandarin, single-spaced（封面與參考文獻不計；圖表引文不佔頁數）
  • Introduction = conceptual framework (1 page) + brief intro（≤1.5 頁）：談什麼、為何重要、該學到什麼
  • Conclusion + References（APA 7、至少 5 篇，以同儕審查期刊為主；Wikipedia 不算）
  • Teamwork sheet 分工表（每人至少一項實質工作）
  • GenAI declaration + transcripts 揭露聲明＋對話紀錄附件

Oral 口頭（15%）

  • PowerPoint（not PDF/Canva），頁碼、版面專業易讀
  • 40–60 min per group; every student presents ≥5 min 每人至少上台五分鐘
  • Other groups must raise ≥1 question 其他組至少提一個問題
  • Inter- and intra-group peer grading 組間與組內互評

Recommended analytical spine (from U12): diagnose with course frameworks → prescribe with feasibility → stress-test against quality, money, ethics and law. Reference readings for each topic are listed on e-Course.

建議分析主軸（見 U12）：用課程框架診斷 → 開出可行處方 → 以品質、財務、倫理法規做壓力測試。各主題的參考文獻清單見 e-Course。
