Hospital Management Profile

醫院管理側寫

Take one real Taiwanese hospital (the one you found in Week 1's exercise) and profile it like a manager would: its identity, its skeleton, its people levers, and its thinking risks.

選一家真實的台灣醫院（第一週練習找的那家），像管理者一樣為它做側寫：它的身分、它的骨架、它的用人槓桿、它的思考風險。

Submit 繳交: e-Course2, PDF, due 17:00 · late −20%/day, 0 after 3 days · individual work, discussion welcome · disclose AI use 個人作業，可討論；使用 AI 須揭露

✎

Tasks

題目（共 100 分）

Part 1 · Identity 機構身分（20 分）

From the hospital's website, quote its mission, vision, and values (culture if published). In 5–8 sentences, judge whether they are consistent with each other and with what the hospital actually appears to do. Cite the URL and access date.

從醫院官網引出其使命、願景、價值（若有公布文化亦列出）。以 5–8 句評論三者是否彼此一致、且與醫院實際作為相符。附網址與查閱日期。

Part 2 · Skeleton 組織骨架（30 分）

Draw the hospital's organizational chart (from public info; simplify to 2–3 levels). Identify the structure type(s) from W1 — functional, matrix, service line, etc. — and give one advantage and one risk of this design for this hospital specifically.

畫出該院組織圖（依公開資訊，簡化為 2–3 層）。判斷其屬 W1 的哪種結構（功能式、矩陣、服務線……可複合），並針對「這家醫院」寫出此設計的一項優勢與一項風險。

Part 3 · People levers 用人槓桿（30 分）

Design one employee-motivation initiative for a department of your choice, explicitly using two different motivation theories from W3 (e.g., Herzberg + expectancy). State the target behavior, the mechanism per theory, and one metric to check in 6 months.

為自選部門設計一項員工激勵方案，明確運用 W3 的兩個不同動機理論（如 Herzberg＋期望理論）。寫出目標行為、每個理論對應的機制，與六個月後檢核的一個指標。

Part 4 · Thinking risks 思考風險（20 分）

Describe one plausible decision-bias scenario for this hospital's management (W4 catalog: overconfidence, confirmation, availability, familiarity, attribution error, halo…), and one concrete de-biasing mechanism (checklist, devil's advocate, structured criteria…).

描述一個此醫院管理層可能發生的決策偏誤情境（W4 目錄：過度自信、確認、可得性、熟悉性、歸因謬誤、月暈……），並提出一個具體的防偏機制（查檢表、唱反調角色、結構化準則……）。

Format 格式 3–4 pages PDF, bilingual or English. Include the org chart as a figure. Quote sources for every fact about the hospital. 3–4 頁 PDF，雙語或英文。組織圖以圖呈現。醫院相關事實均須附來源。
