§1

The Course on One Map

一張地圖看整門課

Every management question can be located on three axes: people (how humans behave and are led), system (how the organization and its environment are wired), and guardrails (what quality, money and law permit). Expand each module.

每個管理問題都能定位在三條軸上：人（人如何行動與被領導）、系統（組織與環境如何接線）、護欄（品質、財務與法律允許什麼）。展開各模組。

Six functions and three skills (U1) tell you what managers do; leadership models and styles (U2) tell you how to move people; motivation theories (U3) tell you why they move; OB and biases (U4) warn you how your own thinking fails. Case use: any staffing, morale or leadership problem starts here.

六大功能與三種技能（U1）說明管理者「做什麼」；領導模型與風格（U2）說明「如何帶動人」；動機理論（U3）說明「人為何而動」；組織行為與偏誤（U4）提醒「你自己的思考會怎麼失靈」。個案應用：所有人力、士氣、領導問題從這裡下手。

Providers and scopes of practice (U5) define who may do what; hospital governance, integration and safety culture (U6) define the org chart and its failure modes; public health and Taiwan's system history (U7) set the population context. Case use: check whether the problem is really a structure or scope-of-practice problem in disguise.

醫事職類與執業範圍（U5）定義誰能做什麼；醫院治理、整合與安全文化（U6）定義組織圖與其失靈模式；公共衛生與台灣體系史（U7）給出族群脈絡。個案應用：先檢查問題是否其實是「結構」或「職類範圍」問題偽裝的。

QI methods (U8) measure and fix the process; payment systems (U9) explain the incentives everyone is actually responding to; ethics and law (U10) mark the boundaries no solution may cross. Case use: every prescription must pass all three gates — measurable, incentive-compatible, legal-ethical.

品質改善（U8）測量並修復流程；支付制度（U9）解釋所有人真正回應的誘因；倫理與法律（U10）標出任何方案不可跨越的界線。個案應用：每個處方都要過三關——可測量、誘因相容、合法合倫理。

GenAI (U11) is this decade's change-management test: real benefits, six named risks, hard red lines. ESG reframes the whole course as a sustainability question — E maps to operations, S to your people-and-equity units, G to board governance. Case use: technology-adoption cases get the traffic-light + five-questions framework; any case can add an ESG stress-test dimension.

GenAI（U11）是這十年的變革管理考題：效益真實、六大風險有名有姓、紅線清楚。ESG 單元則把整門課重新框成永續問題——E 對應營運、S 對應人與公平各單元、G 對應董事會治理。個案應用：科技導入個案套紅綠燈＋五問；任何個案都可以加一層 ESG 壓力測試。

§2

The Case Method: Diagnose → Prescribe → Stress-Test

個案分析法：診斷 → 處方 → 壓力測試

1 · Diagnose 診斷

  • State the problem in one measurable sentence 用一句可測量的話陳述問題
  • Name the frameworks that explain it (≥2 units of this course) 指出能解釋它的課程框架（至少兩個單元）
  • Separate symptoms from root causes — fishbone if needed 區分症狀與根因，必要時畫魚骨圖

2 · Prescribe 處方

  • One primary intervention + implementation steps 一個主方案＋導入步驟
  • Who pays, who resists, what incentives shift 誰出錢、誰抗拒、哪些誘因改變
  • Metrics and a check date (FOCUS-PDCA logic) 指標與查核時點

3 · Stress-test 壓力測試

  • Quality: which Donabedian element does it move? 品質面動到哪個要素
  • Money: survives the payment system? 財務面撐得過支付制度嗎
  • Ethics & law: four principles + Taiwan statutes 倫理法律面：四原則與台灣法規
Worked example
示範 “ER nurses quit at 2× the hospital average.” Diagnose: Herzberg (hygiene collapse: violence, shifts) + attribution check (is management blaming persons for a situation?). Prescribe: security upgrade + self-scheduling pilot + coaching for the head nurse; metric = 6-month retention. Stress-test: Donabedian structure→outcome; costs vs one-nurse replacement (US$22–64k, U6); no legal barrier, justice favors night staff. That is a complete 10-minute story. 「急診護理師離職率是全院兩倍。」診斷：Herzberg（保健因子崩壞：暴力、班表）＋歸因檢查（管理層是否把情境怪到個人？）。處方：保全升級＋自主排班試辦＋護理長教練輔導；指標＝六個月留任率。壓力測試：Donabedian 結構→結果；成本對比一位護理師的替換費（U6：2.2–6.4 萬美元）；無法律障礙、正義原則有利夜班。這就是完整的十分鐘故事。
§3

Case-Study Presentation Logistics

個案發表規則（依課程大綱）

Format 形式

  • PowerPoint only（not PDF/Canva）僅限 PPT
  • 40–60 min per group; every member ≥5 min 每組 40–60 分鐘、每人至少 5 分鐘
  • Written paper due the week BEFORE, by COB 書面於發表前一週 COB 前繳交

Rubric 評分（大綱頁 §5）

  • Paper text 文本 20% · Integration & depth 整合深度 20%
  • Neutral voice 中立 20% · Synthesis 綜整 15% · Conclusions 結論 15%
  • Oral 15% + paper 25% of course grade 口頭與書面共佔學期 40%

Rules 規則

  • Other groups ask ≥1 question 他組至少提問一次
  • Inter- and intra-group peer grading 組間組內互評
  • Teamwork sheet + GenAI declaration with transcripts 分工表＋AI 揭露與對話紀錄
