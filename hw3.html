A Quality Improvement Proposal

品質改善計畫書

Write the one-page QI proposal a hospital quality committee would actually accept — plus a cost-sharing computation and an ethics check, because in real life the three always arrive together.

寫一份品質委員會真的會收的一頁 QI 計畫書——外加一題部分負擔計算與一段倫理檢核，因為真實世界裡這三件事總是一起出現。

Ungraded practice 不計分: self-study rehearsal — the fishbone + FOCUS-PDCA + ethics check is a mini version of the graded case-study analysis. 不計分排練——魚骨圖＋FOCUS-PDCA＋倫理檢核正是個案分析的迷你版。

✎

Tasks

題目（自評配分 100）

Part 1 · Fishbone 魚骨圖（20 分）

Pick one recurring hospital process problem (waits, delays, errors, no-shows — fictional or from public reports). Draw a cause-and-effect diagram with at least four branches (people, methods, equipment, environment…) and ≥8 candidate causes.

選一個反覆發生的醫院流程問題（等候、延遲、錯誤、爽約——可虛構或取材公開報導）。畫魚骨圖，至少四條大骨（人、方法、設備、環境……）、八個以上候選原因。

Part 2 · FOCUS-PDCA plan 改善計畫（40 分）

Write a one-page FOCUS-PDCA proposal for the same problem: every letter gets 1–3 sentences; U must state a measurable baseline (invent a plausible one), S selects ONE intervention, and C names the metric and check date. Follow the W8 simulator's logic.

為同一問題寫一頁 FOCUS-PDCA 計畫：每個字母 1–3 句；U 要有可測量的基線（可合理虛構）、S 只選「一個」介入、C 要寫出指標與查核日。邏輯比照 W8 模擬器。

Part 3 · Cost-sharing computation 部分負擔計算（20 分）

A patient's bill is $36,000. Copay $400; deductible remaining $8,000; coinsurance 15%. Compute the patient's and insurer's shares, showing each step (copay → deductible → coinsurance). Then one paragraph: which of the three tools best fights moral hazard for minor visits, and why?

病人帳單 36,000 元。定額 400 元；未達自負額 8,000 元；定率 15%。逐步（定額→自負額→定率）計算病人與保險人各付多少。再以一段說明：對「輕症」就醫，三種工具中哪個最能抑制道德風險？為什麼？

Part 4 · Ethics check 倫理檢核（20 分）

Apply two of the four principles (W10) to your Part 2 intervention: could it harm anyone (non-maleficence)? Does it distribute burdens fairly (justice)? Does it respect patient or staff autonomy? 5–8 sentences.

用四原則中的兩個（W10）檢核你 Part 2 的介入：會傷害任何人嗎（不傷害）？負擔分配公平嗎（正義）？尊重病人或員工自主嗎？5–8 句。

Format 格式 3 pages PDF max (fishbone counts as a figure). No real patient data — fictional numbers are fine if plausible and labeled. 至多 3 頁 PDF（魚骨圖算一張圖）。不得使用真實病人資料——合理且標示為虛構的數字即可。
