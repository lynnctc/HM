Health Care Management 101

醫療管理總論與課程導覽

Hospitals don't run themselves. Behind every safe ward and smooth clinic is a manager doing six kinds of work with three kinds of skill, inside a structure someone had to design. This week we define health care management, meet the manager's functions and competencies, and tour the organizational structures that hold a hospital together.

醫院不會自己運轉。每個安全的病房、順暢的門診背後，都有管理者在一套被設計出來的結構裡，用三種技能做六種工作。本週定義醫療管理，認識管理者的功能與職能，並巡覽支撐一家醫院的各種組織結構。

§1

What Is Management — and Why Health Care Needs It

什麼是管理？為什麼醫療特別需要它

Management is the process — made of social and technical functions and activities — occurring within organizations for the purpose of accomplishing predetermined objectives through human and other resources. The key phrase: managers work through others. A manager who does everything personally is not managing.

管理是一個過程：由社會性與技術性的功能和活動組成，發生在組織之中，目的是透過人力與其他資源達成預定目標。關鍵字是「透過他人」完成工作——凡事親力親為的人，不是在管理。

Health care managers work in direct care settings (hospitals, clinics — care is delivered to patients) and non-direct care settings (insurers, pharma, consulting, equipment firms — they support the ones who deliver care). The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics projects employment of medical and health services managers to grow about 28.5% from 2023 to 2033 — among the fastest of all occupations. (The slide deck cites the older 2012–22 estimate of 23%; the direction is the same: this field keeps hiring.)

醫療管理者的職場分為直接照護場域（醫院、診所——直接服務病人）與非直接照護場域（保險、藥廠、顧問、醫材——支援提供照護的機構）。美國勞統局推估 2023–2033 年醫療服務管理職將成長約 28.5%，居所有職業前列（投影片為舊版 2012–22 的 23%，方向一致：這個領域一直缺人）。

The six management functions 六大管理功能

Planning 規劃

  • Set direction, priorities, and performance targets定方向、定優先順序、定績效目標

Organizing 組織

  • Design the unit: positions, reporting lines, distribution of authority設計單位：職位、報告線、權責分配

Staffing 人力配置

  • Acquire, develop and retain the workforce取得、發展、留住人力

Controlling 控制

  • Monitor performance and take corrective action監測績效、採取矯正行動

Directing 領導執行

  • Initiate action through leadership, motivation and communication以領導、激勵與溝通啟動行動

Decision making 決策

  • Weigh benefits and drawbacks of alternatives — underlies all the others權衡各方案利弊——貫穿其他五項功能

§2

Three Skills, Many Levels

三種技能、多個層級

A competency is having the requisite ability or qualities to perform a function. Katz's classic trio still organizes the field: conceptual, technical, and interpersonal skills. Their mix shifts as you move up: senior managers lean conceptual; first-line managers lean technical.

「職能」指具備執行某功能所需的能力或特質。Katz 的經典三分法至今仍是主軸：概念性、技術性、人際性技能。層級越高越吃概念性技能；基層主管則偏重技術性技能。

Conceptual skills 概念性技能

  • Critically analyze and solve complex problems批判分析、解決複雜問題
  • e.g. design a strategy to cut patient complaints about food service例：設計降低膳食客訴的策略

Technical skills 技術性技能

  • Expertise to perform specific work tasks執行特定工作的專業能力
  • e.g. build a new incentive compensation program; modify a staffing model例：建立獎金制度、修改排班模型

Interpersonal skills 人際性技能

  • Communicate and work well with peers, bosses, subordinates與同儕、上司、部屬有效溝通合作
  • e.g. counsel an under-performing employee例：輔導績效不佳的員工

Management positions run from lower level through middle to senior; authority is delegated downward. Line managers supervise other employees; staff managers advise and support without routinely supervising anyone. And management happens at three focal levels — self, unit/team, organization-wide: you cannot run a ward if you cannot run your own calendar.

管理職涵蓋基層、中階到高階，職權由上往下授權。直線主管督導部屬；幕僚主管提供建議與支援、不例行督導他人。管理也發生在三個焦點層次——自我、單位／團隊、全組織：連自己行事曆都管不好的人，管不了一個病房。

§4

Culture, Values, Mission, Vision

文化、價值、使命、願景

Culture 文化

  • Beliefs, attitudes and behaviors shared among members — “how we do things here”成員共享的信念、態度與行為——「我們這裡的做事方式」

Values 價值

  • Principles the organization believes in, guiding purpose, goals and daily behavior組織信奉的原則，引導宗旨、目標與日常行為

Mission 使命

  • The organization's purpose — what it seeks to achieve now組織存在的目的——現在要達成什麼

Vision 願景

  • The desired future state — what it wants to be known for期望的未來樣貌——希望以什麼被記得

§5

Six Hats of the Health Care Manager

管理者的六頂帽子

Beyond the daily functions, Chapter 1 assigns the manager six ongoing roles. Expand each one.

在日常功能之外，第一章給管理者六個持續性的角色。逐一展開來看。

Recruit, retain, train and develop skilled staff — organizations compete for the best people. Engagement strategies: training programs, leadership development, engagement surveys, continuing education, job enrichment.

招募、留任、訓練並發展人才——機構彼此競逐最好的人。敬業度策略：訓練方案、領導力發展、敬業度調查、繼續教育、工作豐富化。

High-performance organizations are results-oriented. Studer's pillars: people, service, quality, finance, growth. Stakeholders — insurers, governments, consumer groups — increasingly demand safe, convenient, low-cost, high-quality care.

高績效組織以結果為導向。Studer 的五大支柱：人員、服務、品質、財務、成長。利害關係人（保險人、政府、消費者團體）愈來愈「要求」安全、便利、低成本、高品質的照護。

Leadership development: structured courses, mentoring, 360° feedback, coaching, job enlargement. Succession planning ensures staff can move up when managers retire or leave — needed at all levels now, not just the C-suite.

領導力發展：結構化課程、師徒制、360 度回饋、教練、工作擴大化。接班規劃確保主管退休或離職時有人能接上——現在各層級都需要，不只高階。

Two types (Hamel): management innovation — changing how the work of managing is done; operational innovation — changing business processes: customer service, procurement, care coordination, clinical procedures.

兩種創新（Hamel）：管理創新——改變「管理工作本身」怎麼做；營運創新——改變業務流程：顧客服務、採購、照護協調、臨床作業。

A structured approach based on knowledge of performance gaps. Four steps: identify the need → plan the implementation → implement → evaluate. Remember the order; the quiz will ask.

以績效缺口為根據的結構化方法。四步驟：確認變革需求 → 規劃導入 → 執行 → 評估。順序要記住，測驗會考。

Especially for senior managers: track policy under consideration that affects your organization. Staff may monitor it for you — but the manager who stops paying attention loses opportunities.

對高階主管尤其重要：追蹤影響機構的政策動向。即使有幕僚代為監測，不關心政策的主管會錯失先機。

Taiwan Spotlight 台灣視角

Taiwan's National Development Council publishes workforce supply-demand projections for the health care service industry — the same manpower-planning logic your textbook describes, applied locally: profession-by-profession supply pipelines (licensing exam pass rates, graduating cohorts) matched against projected demand. Health administration graduates staff hospitals' administration centers, the NHI Administration, health bureaus, and the growing long-term-care sector.

國發會定期發布醫療保健服務業的人力供需推估——正是教科書人力規劃邏輯的在地版：逐職類盤點供給管道（國考及格率、畢業人數）對照需求推估。醫管畢業生的去處包括醫院行政管理中心、健保署、衛生局與快速成長的長照產業。
