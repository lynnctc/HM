Public Health & Taiwan's Health System

公共衛生與台灣醫療體系

Hospitals treat the sick one at a time; public health keeps whole populations from getting sick in the first place — and it is, famously, the best-kept secret in the health system. This week: what public health does, two centuries of world and Taiwan history in two clickable timelines, the difference between equality and equity, and Taiwan's newest health profession, the certified public health specialist.

醫院一次治一個病人；公共衛生讓整個族群一開始就不生病——它也是醫療體系裡「最被埋沒的秘密」。本週內容：公共衛生做什麼、兩條可點擊的時間軸走完世界與台灣兩百年公衛史、平等與公平的差別，以及台灣最新的醫事專業——公共衛生師。

§1

The Best-Kept Secret

最被埋沒的秘密

Public health is the organized effort of society to keep populations healthy — population-based medicine focused on prevention rather than treatment. The CDC's ten great achievements of 1900–1999 (vaccination, motor-vehicle safety, safer workplaces, infectious-disease control, fewer heart-disease deaths, safer food, healthier mothers and babies, family planning, fluoridation, tobacco control) added roughly 25 of the 30 years of life expectancy gained in that century.

公共衛生是社會為維持族群健康所做的有組織努力——以族群為對象的醫學，重預防而非治療。CDC 評選的 1900–1999 十大公衛成就（疫苗、交通安全、職場安全、傳染病防治、心血管死亡下降、食品安全、婦幼健康、家庭計畫、飲水加氟、菸害防制）貢獻了該世紀約 30 年平均餘命增幅中的 25 年。

§2

Two Hundred Years in Two Timelines

兩條時間軸的兩百年

1700s Ramazzini: Diseases of Workers 工人的疾病
The Italian physician's De Morbis Artificum Diatriba described causes and symptoms of 52 occupational diseases — the founding classic of occupational medicine.義大利醫師的著作描述 52 種職業病的病因與症狀——職業醫學最早經典。
1848–1854 Cholera, John Snow & the first Public Health Act 霍亂、斯諾與第一部公衛法
Britain's cholera epidemics led John Snow to trace transmission to contaminated water — founding modern epidemiology — and Parliament to pass the world's first Public Health Act (1848).英國霍亂大流行中，John Snow 證明水污染傳播病菌，成為現代流行病學之父；國會 1848 年通過世界第一部以公共衛生為名的法案。
1881 Bismarck's social insurance 俾斯麥社會保險
Germany created the world's first comprehensive social insurance for sickness, disability and occupational injury — the ancestor of every NHI, including Taiwan's.德國建立世界第一個涵蓋疾病、殘廢、勞動災害的全面社會保險——包括台灣健保在內所有社會保險的始祖。
1872–1933 America professionalizes 美國公衛專業化
APHA founded 1872; Public Health Service 1912; first school of public health (Johns Hopkins) 1920; the New Deal expanded federal public health massively during the Depression.1872 美國公共衛生學會；1912 公共衛生服務部；1920 第一所公衛學院（Johns Hopkins）；新政期間聯邦公衛職能大幅擴張。
1948 WHO & the NHS 世界衛生組織與英國 NHS
The World Health Organization and Britain's National Health Service were both born in 1948; the U.S. added Medicare and Medicaid in 1964–65.WHO 與英國國民保健署同於 1948 年誕生；美國 1964–65 開辦 Medicare 與 Medicaid。
1978 Alma-Ata Declaration 阿拉木圖宣言
Primary health care became the backbone of national health strategy: health promotion, disease prevention, equity — “Health for All.”初級健康照護成為國家健康策略骨架：健康促進、疾病預防、健康平等——「全民均健」。
1986 Ottawa Charter 渥太華憲章
Five strategies for health promotion: healthy public policy, supportive environments, community action, personal skills, reoriented health services.健康促進五大策略：健康的公共政策、支持性環境、強化社區行動、發展個人技能、調整健康服務取向。
1865–1895 Medical missionaries 醫療傳道時代
Maxwell, Mackay and Landsborough brought Western medicine; Sin-Lau (新樓), Changhua Christian (彰基) and Mackay (馬偕) became the era's three great private hospitals.馬雅各、馬偕、蘭大衛引進西方醫學；新樓、彰基、馬偕成為三大私立醫院。
1895–1945 Japanese era: sanitation state 日據時代：衛生建設
Port quarantine, case reporting, vaccination, waterworks and sewers beat plague and controlled smallpox, cholera, malaria. The Medical School (1899, forerunner of NTU Medicine) trained 2,800+ physicians. Life expectancy rose from 29/30 (M/F, 1906) to 42/47 (1940).港口檢疫、通報、預防接種、自來水與下水道撲滅鼠疫並控制天花霍亂瘧疾。總督府醫學校（1899，台大醫學院前身）培育逾 2,800 名醫師。平均壽命從 1906 年的 29／30 歲（男／女）升至 1940 年的 42／47 歲。
1945–1971 Health stations & malaria eradication 衛生所與根除瘧疾
360 health stations built in five years — one per township. The malaria program (research center 1946, DDT campaign from 1952) earned WHO's malaria-eradication certificate in 1965 — Taiwan's greatest infectious-disease victory. The provincial health system was capped by the Department of Health in 1971.五年內設 360 間衛生所，一鄉鎮一所。瘧疾防治（1946 研究中心、1952 起 DDT 家戶噴灑）於 1965 年獲 WHO 根除瘧疾證明——傳染病防治最卓越成就。1971 年成立行政院衛生署。
1959– Family planning & MCH 家庭計畫與婦幼衛生
Family planning launched 1959 (from birth control then, to birth encouragement now); public health nurses took prenatal guidance, vaccination and cancer screening into communities; iodized salt beat goiter, piped water beat blackfoot disease.1959 年推家庭計畫（從節育到今日的催生）；公衛護士深入社區做產前指導、接種與癌症防治；食鹽加碘制伏甲狀腺腫、自來水終結烏腳病。
1995 National Health Insurance 全民健保開辦
Single-payer, compulsory social insurance pooling the old civil-servant, labor and farmer schemes; coverage jumped from 58% to over 99%. Deep dive in Week 9.單一保險人、強制性社會保險，整併公勞農保；納保率由 58% 升至逾 99%。第 9 週深入。
2013–2020 MOHW & the Public Health Specialists Act 衛福部與公衛師法
The Department of Health became the Ministry of Health and Welfare (2013). After SARS, MERS and COVID-19 showed the need, the Public Health Specialists Act was promulgated on 2020-06-03 — see §5.2013 年衛生署升格衛生福利部。歷經 SARS、MERS、COVID-19，公共衛生師法於 109 年 6 月 3 日公布施行——見 §5。
§3

Equality Is Not Equity

平等不等於公平

Determinants of health — personal, social, economic and environmental factors — shape health long before anyone reaches a hospital. Health disparities are differences that are avoidable, unjust and socially structured. Equality gives everyone the same box to stand on; equity gives each person the boxes they need to see over the fence — and ultimately removes the fence: poverty, discrimination, and power imbalances. The starkest yardstick: life expectancy at birth can differ by decades between settings — “from womb to tomb.”

健康決定因素——個人、社會、經濟與環境——早在任何人踏進醫院之前就決定了健康。健康不平等指可避免、不公義且由社會結構造成的差異。平等是給每個人同一個箱子墊腳；公平是給每個人「他需要的箱子數」讓他看得到球賽——最終目標是拆掉圍籬本身：貧窮、歧視與權力失衡。最鮮明的量尺：不同環境的出生平均餘命可相差數十年——「從子宮到墳墓」。

The Epidemiologist's Toolkit

流行病學工具箱

Epidemiology investigates hypotheses about disease causation; surveillance systems collect the data; the epidemiologist is the disease detective. Disease occurs when the triad — agent, host, environment — falls out of ecological balance, and outbreaks propagate along a chain of infection that public health tries to break at its weakest link. §5

Taiwan Spotlight: the Public Health Specialist 公共衛生師

台灣視角：公共衛生師法

Promulgated 2020-06-03 after decades of advocacy (and the push of SARS → COVID-19), the Public Health Specialists Act created Taiwan's first non-medical licensed health profession. Design: “professional but not exclusive” (專業不專屬) — only certified specialists may use the title, but the listed work may also be done by medical personnel, government programs, or research projects. Scope: community/site-based planning, implementation and evaluation of environmental risk, epidemic investigation and control, population health promotion, and food-safety risk programs.

歷經數十年倡議（加上 SARS 到 COVID-19 的推力），公共衛生師法於 109 年 6 月 3 日公布施行，創設台灣第一個非醫事人員的健康專業證照。設計：「專業不專屬」——僅領證者可用公衛師名稱，但法定業務也可由醫事人員、政府方案或研究計畫執行。業務範圍：社區與場域的環境健康風險、疫病調查防治、民眾健康狀態調查與健康促進、食品安全風險等方案之規劃、推動或評估。
